Popular Sources

Since 2015, Dr Matthews is one of the University of Adelaide’s top 10 most read authors on The Conversation. Demonstrating the reach of his work, he has been translated into numerous languages include German, Indonesian and Arabic and amassed a following of over 1,000,000 readers.
